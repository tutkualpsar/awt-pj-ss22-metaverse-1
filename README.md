## Descrpition

Hello! Welcome to our vitual clinic consultation office!

**[Please join us on the Glitch platform to fully experience it!](https://glitch.com/~metaverse1)**

## Getting Started

### Dependencies

- Operation Systems: MacOS, Windows
- Web Browsers: Google Chrome, Microsoft Edge, Mozilla Firefox

### Installing

You don't need to do any application installation. Please just enter the link above and click the "visit" bottom to start it.

### Executing program

Once you enter into the waiting room, click and hold the left mouse button at any point on the screen and drag to move around the scene with the mouse.

You can see in the middle of the scene, there is a black circle. Some patients and a receptionist could be found in the room as well.

Once the black circle is positioned on the yellow arrow, you can jump to the next room with one more click. 

There are three spaces, waiting room, the offfice of the clinician and the lab.

Please note that space jumping is only possible when the black circle cursor is inside the yellow arrow area!

Enjoy the trip!
